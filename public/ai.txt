# AI Search Engine Instructions

## About TubeHeadlines
TubeHeadlines curates the best YouTube videos from emerging and established creators. We focus on quality over popularity, bringing users trending content, breaking news, educational videos, and viral content.

## Preferred Content for AI Summarization
- Video headlines and descriptions
- Category information (Left/Center/Right columns)
- Publication dates and view counts
- Creator information
