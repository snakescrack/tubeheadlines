Frequently Asked Questions

What is TubeHeadlines?

TubeHeadlines is a curated discovery platform for YouTube videos. we filter through thousands of daily uploads to find the most important headlines, trending stories, and high-quality content from both established and emerging creators.

Do you host the videos on your site?

No. All videos on TubeHeadlines are embedded directly from YouTube's official embed player. This ensures that the original creators receive 100% of the views, ad revenue, and engagement directly on their YouTube channels.

How do you choose which videos to feature?

Our curation process combines algorithmic tracking of trending topics with human editorial review. We look for high production value, unique perspectives, and stories that are currently driving conversation across the platform.

Are the creator tools really free?

Yes. All tools in our Tools Hub—including the Income Calculator, Viral Idea Generator, and SEO Scorecard—are 100% free to use. No sign-ups or subscriptions required.

How can I get my channel featured?

You can submit your channel or a specific video for review through our submission page. While we can't feature every submission, we prioritize high-quality content from creators who are providing value to their audience.

Is my data safe on your site?

Yes. We do not collect personal information like names or emails. We only track anonymous analytics to improve the user experience. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
