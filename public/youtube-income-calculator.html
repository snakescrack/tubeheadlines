Frequently Asked Questions

How is YouTube RPM calculated?

RPM (Revenue Per Mille) is the amount of money you earn per 1,000 video views. It varies by niche, with Finance/Tech typically having higher RPMs than Vlogging/Gaming.

How accurate is this YouTube money calculator?

This calculator uses industry-standard RPM ranges for 2024 to provide a realistic estimate. However, actual earnings can vary based on viewer location, ad blockers, and video length.

Does YouTube pay for every view?

No, YouTube only pays for monetized playbacks where an ad is shown. This calculator assumes a standard monetization rate typical for established channels.
